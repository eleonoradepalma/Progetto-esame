ERIKA SORBELLO

HI! MY NAME IS ERIKA

I'm an Italian phorographer

My interest in photography started when i was 13, thanks to an old camera belonging to my parents. A few years laters, my first real camera arrived.

After more than 2 years of working, and due to my parents’ sacrificies, i managed to enroll in IED (European Institue of Design) and attend the Photography Course.

"I loves capturing people, especially
making them see their own beauty”

During my studies I discovered my passion for beauty and editorial portraits. Then I began my first steps in studio photography, and I’m now also specializing in photo retouching. Over the years I’ve mastered my skills in events photography such as for fairs, baptisms, parties and weddings. Moreover, I also parecipated in reportage projects that tell stories about society, humanity, people and culture.
